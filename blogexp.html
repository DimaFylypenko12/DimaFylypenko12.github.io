Study choice

From a young age, I embarked on a remarkable journey into the world of programming, and this voyage has profoundly influenced my life. My passion for coding began during my childhood, where I had the opportunity to explore various programming languages and technologies. In this essay, I will share my experiences with JavaScript, C++, C#, Python, HTML, CSS, NodeJS, working in teams, and creating desktop applications, reflecting on the valuable lessons learned and the impact on my personal and professional growth.
My programming journey took flight with Python.It was the first language I dabbled in, thanks to its accessibility for beginners. I was fascinated by how a few lines of code could bring interactive elements to life on a webpage. Python laid the foundation for my understanding of the logic behind programming. Python's simplicity and versatility won my heart, and I began using it for data analysis. Then I statred being interested in web development and learnt HTML and CSS.
As I grew older, I delved into more robust languages like C++, C#, JavaScript. JavaScript (also using NodeJS) allowed me to create Desctop applications. With JavaScript I discovered OOP(Object oriented Programming).
